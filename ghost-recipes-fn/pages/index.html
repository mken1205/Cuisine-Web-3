On the Ghost Kitchen

Why the best food has no address

Ghost kitchens are not a trend. They are a return to something older than dining rooms — the idea that food's purpose is nourishment and pleasure, not performance. The best meals I've eaten were from windows, carts, and hands I never saw twice.
